Amazing Christmas fireworks show!

Posted on 12/23/18

Today our local park held the 52nd annual Christmas fireworks extravaganza! All I can say is WOW! This year the townhip really went above and beyond with their fireworks. They had so many colors, designs, and patterns, it really was a joy to watch. At the finale, there was even a giant firework that was shaped like Santa! I look forward to this every year, and now I am more excited than ever for what the township has in store for us in next years' celebration!
